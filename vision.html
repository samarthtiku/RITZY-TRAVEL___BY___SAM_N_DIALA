Vision Accessibility Feature

Magnifiers and Visual Aids

Our vision accessibility feature includes magnifiers and visual aids to enhance your browsing experience.

Key Features:

  • Magnification: Zoom in on text and images for better visibility.
  • Contrast Adjustment: Customize the color contrast to suit your preferences.
  • Highlighting: Highlight important elements on the page.

How to Use Magnifiers:

To use magnifiers, simply click on the "Vision" option in our accessibility widget, and the magnification tools will be activated. You can then adjust settings based on your needs.
